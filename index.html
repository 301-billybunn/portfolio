More About Billy

In late 2018, I quit my job as a public accountant at an international accounting firm to start studying software development full-time.
While studying for my degree at the University of Washington, I was fascinated by economics and business models. And as a financial statement auditor, I got an insider’s view on how businesses are run wnd worked alongside company executives. I always enjoyed solving problems in teams, but the day-to-day work of a CPA wasn’t for me. I took the advice of some developer friends and started learning how to code. I knew quickly that this is the path for me.
I want to leverage my finance background to be a part of a software company that builds tools that make all our lives easier.
I'm a Northern California transplant, UW graduate, and public accountant turned software engineer. I enjoy complaining about Seattle weather and exploring the great Pacific Northwest with my wonderful wife.
